About Us

Kareo Projects (KP) is a 100% black woman-owned and accredited training consulting company specializing in construction and health & safety training. With over 15 years of industry experience, we are committed to skills development across South Africa.

Senior Director: Keitumetse Beauty Motake

Ms. Motake is an accredited facilitator, assessor, and moderator with over 20 years of experience in training and skills development within the construction sector. She has contributed to major national projects including Medupi, Kusile, and Gautrain.

Our Vision

To provide world-class training services and ensure that health and safety remain paramount within the construction industry through experienced and elegant training delivery.

Our Mission

To spread knowledge and shape South Africa into a better country through continued skills development.
